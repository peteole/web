## Talent Acquisition Manager

<About />

As Ory expands, best-in-class recruiting processes and a filled talent pipeline
play an integral role to the success of our company. This position provides the
opportunity to set up a new team and to build the company with us. Leave a
footprint!

### Overview

- **Position type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany

### What you'll do

- Design and implement a recruiting strategy that is in line with the overall
  business strategy.
- Define, implement and improve an end-to-end recruiting process (from sourcing
  to onboarding) for the various departments that ensures a positive candidate
  experience throughout the whole process and fast decision making.
- Ensure that hiring needs are being evaluated and forecasted together with the
  executive team in one hiring plan.
- Ensure the filling of requested positions.
- Define and implement interview standards.
- Generate a talent pipeline; build and maintain a network of potential
  candidates through pro-active market research and on-going relationship
  management stay up-to-date on labor legislation and changes in regulations.
