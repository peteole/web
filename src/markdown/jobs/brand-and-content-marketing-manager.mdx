## Brand & Content Marketing Manager

<About />

As a Brand & Content Marketing Manager at Ory, you will be responsible for
generating new customers and leads for Ory Cloud through owned and earned media
channels. You will be responsible for strategy, execution excellence and
accountability reporting. You will play a key role in the development of brand
and content marketing and decide on key success factors. With Product Management
and your Marketing colleagues you will regularly assess the product and the
market.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location:** Munich, Germany
- **Components:** Google Analytics, Hubspot, CMS Plattforms, Figma (or similar)

### What you'll do

- Creation of the brand & content marketing strategy and goals. This includes
  brand communication guidelines, action plan, budgeting and analytics.
- Design and implementation of all brand & content marketing measures and
  campaigns.
- Evaluate and decide on software and tools for use in brand & content
  marketing.
- Building and optimizing relevant paid and earned media channels such as social
  media networks, email marketing, blog and online communities.
- Creative design of brand assets, content, landing pages and related analytics
  and monitoring.
- Create communication calendars and brand & content response reports.
- Conduct regular brand insights sessions for your Ory colleagues.
- Participate in strategy sessions with Ory management.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Fluent in written and spoken English.
- A strong passion for the internet and data privacy, security and transparency.
- A strong understanding of customer needs and a drive to provide them with
  sustainable solutions.
- Two to three years of professional experience in digital marketing.
- An excellent understanding of brand communication and content strategy.
- Hands-on experience with analytics software and CMS systems.
- A strong affinity for brands and strong communication skills.
- Strong interest in marketing trends and best practices.
- A basic understanding of web technologies such as the HTTP protocol, HTML and
  Javascript.
- Ideally marketing experience in a SaaS, B2B or software environment.
- Working with design tools like Figma is an advantage.
